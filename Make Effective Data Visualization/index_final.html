Graphical Representation of Titanic Survival

The stacked chart shows the survival of passengers acording to their class. It is clear from the graph that first class passengers have the highest survival rate.

The stacked chart shows the survival rate of passengers according to the age group. The childrens have the highest survival rate belonging to age group 0-15.

The stacked bar chart shows the survival of passengers according to the gender and class. The females belonging to the first class have maximum survival rate while the males belong to the third class have very lower survival rate.
